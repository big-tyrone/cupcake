What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. It covers various areas such as algorithms, data structures, programming, and more. Understanding computer science helps us solve problems using technology.

Key Topics in Computer Science

  • Algorithms and Problem Solving
  • Data Structures
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cybersecurity
  • Web Development
  • Software Engineering
